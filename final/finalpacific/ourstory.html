Where Nature Meets Luxury

Pacific Trails Resort was created to offer a unique escape along the California North Coast, where natural beauty and modern comfort come together. Inspired by the surrounding ocean views and peaceful landscape, our resort provides a tranquil setting designed for rest and renewal.

Rooted in a deep connection to nature, every experience is designed to bring guests closer to the coastline while offering thoughtful comfort in every detail. Whether exploring scenic trails, unwinding in a private yurt, or simply taking in the quiet rhythm of the ocean, each moment invites you to slow down, reconnect, and return refreshed.

Our Philosophy

Connection to Nature

We celebrate the beauty of the California coastline by creating experiences that bring guests closer to the natural world.

Thoughtful Comfort

Our spaces are designed to feel both elevated and inviting, blending modern amenities with the warmth of a natural retreat.

Rest & Renewal

We provide an environment where guests can slow down, disconnect, and return feeling refreshed.
